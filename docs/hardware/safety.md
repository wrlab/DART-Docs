# Safety

Indoor flight is risky. This page is intentionally short and strict.

---

## Do not fly if…

- there are people in the scan area
- you cannot immediately take manual control
- your mount is loose or flexible
- lighting is poor and tracking is unstable

---

## Recommended safety setup

- Prop guards (highly recommended)
- Safety perimeter (cones/tape)
- Spotter (recommended)
- Clear takeoff/landing zone

---

## Emergency procedures

- Practice manual takeover before autonomous mode
- Practice landing with low battery
- Know the kill switch / emergency landing sequence
