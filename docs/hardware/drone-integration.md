## Balance & stability

- Perform a hover test in a safe area
- If drift/instability increases:
  - re-check center of gravity
  - reduce payload
  - stiffen the mount (remove flex)

---

## Camera clearance

- Ensure props/landing gear do not dominate the phone’s view
- Avoid occluding the phone’s depth sensor area

---

## Pre-flight checklist (hardware)

- [ ] Mount rigid (no rattle)
- [ ] Phone locked (cannot slide)
- [ ] Camera view clear
- [ ] All straps secure
- [ ] Emergency stop/manual takeover ready
