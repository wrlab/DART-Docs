# Printing

This page covers print settings and practical tips for mount parts.

---

## Material

Recommended:
- PETG (good balance of stiffness and durability)
- ABS/ASA (heat resistant, requires enclosure)

Avoid (for long-term mounts):
- PLA (can deform under heat / sunlight)

---

## Baseline settings (start here)

- Layer height: 0.2 mm
- Perimeters: 4+
- Infill: 30–40%
- Supports: only where needed
- Heatset inserts: optional

---

## Fit check

Before final assembly:
- dry-fit the phone
- verify no sharp edges touch phone body
- verify strap channels are clean

---

## Print orientation tips

- Align layer lines to resist strap tension
- Avoid thin cantilevers without supports
- If the mount vibrates, increase wall thickness and add ribs
