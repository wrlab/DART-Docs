# Web Viewer

The viewer is your main UX surface:

- monitor a scan in real-time
- replay a session archive
- inspect areas of interest

---

## What you should see

During a mission:
- growing reconstruction/map
- UAV trajectory
- synchronized video (if enabled)

After a mission:
- a session you can replay

---

## Browser notes

- Use a modern Chromium-based browser for best results
- If video doesn’t play, confirm codec support and HTTPS requirements (if applicable)
